Live Radio Karaoke transcribes live radio streams in real time and highlights each word
karaoke-style as it is spoken — entirely on your device. No account, no server, no tracking.

Features:
* Real-time streaming speech recognition (sherpa-onnx) for English, French and Mandarin
  Chinese, with code-switching and inline punctuation.
* Karaoke word highlighting synced to playback, with an adjustable sync offset.
* Live summaries and live translation generated on-device by a small language model
  (Gemma 3 1B, or a fully-open alternative you can choose).
* Hundreds of preset stations plus dynamic discovery via the Radio Browser directory.
* Export the full transcript as plain text or SRT subtitles.

All speech recognition and language-model inference run locally on your phone. Model files are
downloaded once on first use and stored on the device. The Gemma model is distributed under
Google's non-free Gemma Terms of Use and is downloaded only after you accept them; a fully-open
model can be used instead.
